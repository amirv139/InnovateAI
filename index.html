What InnovateAI does for your CNC shop

One brain for programming, setup, and continuous improvement across all your machines.

Automatic CNC programming

Upload a drawing or 3D model. The AI proposes operations, toolpaths, feeds, and speeds tailored to your machine and tooling.

Setup co pilot

Clear setup sheets, fixture suggestions, and probing routines so newer operators can run jobs with confidence.

Controller ready programs

Generates machine specific G code with safety checks and simulation so you send proven programs to the floor.

Camera guided setup

We set up a camera at the machine to guide the worker during setup and check every step in real time so tools, fixtures, and work offsets are correct before the job runs.

How the AI copilot fits into your workflow

InnovateAI plugs into the steps you already follow in your shop. No need to replace machines or throw out your CAM.

1

Upload the job

Start with a drawing, 3D model, or repeat job. Add material, quantity, and machine you want to run it on.

2

AI generates the plan

The AI selects tools, operations, and strategies based on your machine, tooling library, and past jobs.

3

Review and tweak

Your programmer or lead machinist reviews toolpaths, makes changes if needed, and approves the job.

4

Send to machine and run

Export controller ready code and setup sheets. The operator follows clear steps and reports back real results.
